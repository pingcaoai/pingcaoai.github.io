Hi, I’m currently a Postdoc Fellow at Maastricht University.
I develop computational and AI methods for assisted reproduction, embryo imaging, reproductive genetics, and preimplantation genetic testing.

Explore

Research Focus

AI-driven reproductive medicine.

Embryo imaging AI

Generative and predictive models for blastocyst-stage embryo images, designed around data realism and clinical usefulness.

LLMs for health literacy

Language models that make reproductive and genetic information clearer, more accessible, and easier to evaluate.

Genomics and PGT

Computational approaches for reproductive genetics, preimplantation genetic testing, and clinically grounded decision support.
